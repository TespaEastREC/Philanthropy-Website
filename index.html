About

phi·LAN·thro·py

The TeSPA East Region is proud to be hosting PhiLANthropy, an intercollegiate charity LAN event driven by and for the collegiate gaming communities. We want to utilize this LAN as an opportunity to unite students across the eastern region that all share similar passions for gaming, as well as raise awareness and money for a charitable cause involved with issues that plague many students and gamers alike.
